Cocktail attire or smart casual. Our celebration will take place indoors but guests are encouraged to explore the property so bring a jacket. Don't forget your dancing shoes!

Weather

Be prepared for cool rainy weather while exploring outside. Historical averages of 50℉(high)/37℉(low). Check out the forecast close to the event time.

More about Edgefield

Historic Edgefield was built in 1911 as the county poor farm. Today the grounds feature a brewery, winery, distillery, movie theatre, golf course and more.

Things to do nearby

The Columbia River Gorge is a designated National Scenic Area with a plethora of recreational activities available.

About Us

Haley immediately moved to San Deigo, CA to pursue a Masters degree in Astronomy while Jordan eventually moved to Eugene, OR to begin Ph.D studies at the University of Oregon. They continued to date long-distance until Haley completed her degree and moved to Eugene in the summer of 2014 and began her current job as the planetarium director at the Eugene Science Center. They finally got engaged in February 2019 back where it all started while they were visiting San Francisco, CA.
